First Time Meeting You

Meeting you was the best thing that ever happened to me






Actually, matagal na kita napansin sa school noon, parang naisipan ko lang na may itsura, ganon. I never thought na I would fall helplessly in love with you. Nung first time kitang nakachat, first thing na lumabas sa isip ko ay "Wow, ang sweet niya naman" and never akong nagkamali. You're the sweetest person I now know.

After our first chat and hearing about you, mas gusto pa kitang makilala. Sinabi mo saakin noon na I can chat you anytime kung may questions pa ako. I remember na pilit talaga akong nagiisip ng maitatanong just to have an excuse to chat with you. I remember you liking my stories on instagram and kung paano ako kinilig doon. I was so happy nung nalaman ko na gusto mo rin ako makachat pa lalo and I have never felt such happiness before. Sobrang saya ko nung first gala natin. To be honest, medyo nahihiya pa ako sayo non. ANG POGI MO KASI. I was so nervous the whole time and hindi ko alam paano ako magaact sa harap mo. Do you remember nung pauwi na tayo non sinulat mo pa plate number nung sinakyan kong tricycle just in case na may mangyari sa akin?

After mo gawin yon, I knew you were the one.
